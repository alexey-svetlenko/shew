PROJECT IDEA

This is NOT a sales page.

It is NOT a typical coaching website.

It is NOT an information page.

I want to create the feeling of the first minutes of a real coaching session.

The visitor should never feel sold to.

Instead, they should feel:

“I feel safe here.”

“I can slow down.”

“I can think.”

“I am not being judged.”

Every screen should feel like a continuation of a quiet conversation.

⸻

MY PHILOSOPHY

I don’t sell answers.

I don’t motivate.

I don’t promise quick transformations.

My work is creating a space where people begin hearing themselves again.

One sentence that MUST appear on the landing page exactly as written:

Я не дам вам готовых ответов. Но могу создать пространство, в котором вы услышите собственные.

This sentence is the core idea of the whole brand.

IMPORTANT UX RULES

The landing page should never feel like marketing.

It should feel like entering a calm room where someone finally isn’t trying to convince you of anything.

The page should breathe.

Silence should become part of the design.

Every transition should feel natural.

Every button should feel like an invitation rather than a call to action.

Design not for conversion.

Design for trust.
